Innate Spellcasting.

The drow cultist\'s innate spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 10). The drow cultist can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components:

While in sunlight, the drow cultist has disadvantage on attack rolls, as well as on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on sight.
