A 15-foot-radius cloud of toxic spores extends out from the vrock. The spores spread around corners. Each creature in that area must succeed on a DC 12+1d6 + 9 Constitution saving throw or become poisoned. While poisoned in this way, a target takes 5 (1d10) poison damage at the start of each of its turns. A target can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success. Emptying a vial of holy water on the target also ends the effect on it.

Stunning Screech (1/Day).

The vrock emits a horrific screech. Each creature within 20 feet of it that can hear it and that isn\'t a demon must succeed on a DC 12+1d6 + 9 Constitution saving throw or be stunned until the end of the vrock\'s next turn.
