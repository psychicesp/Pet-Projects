Multiattack.

The reverser makes three attacks: one with its bite and two with its claws.

Bite.

Melee Weapon Attack: +8+1d6 + 5 to hit, range 5 ft., one creature. Hit: 11 (1d12 + 5) piercing damage plus 6 (1d12) force damage.

Claws.

Melee Weapon Attack: +8+1d6 + 5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d6 + 5) slashing damage plus 7 (2d6) force damage.

Reactions
Reversal.

When a creature the reverser can see within 30 feet of it regains hit points, the reverser reduces the number of hit points regained to 0, and the reverser deals 13 (3d8) force damage to the creature.
