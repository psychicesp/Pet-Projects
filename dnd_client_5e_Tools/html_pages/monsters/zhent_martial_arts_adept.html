Actions
Multiattack.

The adept makes three unarmed strikes or three dart attacks.

Unarmed Strike.

Melee Weapon Attack: +5+1d4 + 3 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d8 + 3) bludgeoning damage. If the target is a creature, the adept can choose one of the following additional effects:

  • The target must succeed on a DC 13+1d4 + 11 Strength saving throw or drop one item it is holding (adept\'s choice).
  • The target must succeed on a DC 13+1d4 + 11 Dexterity saving throw or be knocked prone.
  • The target must succeed on a DC 13+1d4 + 11 Constitution saving throw or be stunned until the end of the adept\'s next turn.

Reactions
Deflect Missile.

In response to being hit by a ranged weapon attack, the adept deflects the missile. The damage it takes from the attack is reduced by 1d10 + 3. If the damage is reduced to 0, the adept catches the missile if it's small enough to hold in one hand and the adept has a hand free.
