Any creature that starts its turn within 10 feet of the plague spreader must make a DC 12+1d4 + 10 Constitution saving throw. Virulent Miasma (1/Day).

The plague spreader releases toxic gas in a 30-foot-radius sphere centered on itself. Each creature in that area must make a DC 12+1d4 + 10 Constitution saving throw, taking 14 (4d6) poison damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. A Humanoid reduced to 0 hit points by this damage dies and rises as a zombie (see its stat block in the Monster Manual) 1 minute later. The zombie acts immediately after the plague spreader in the initiative count.
