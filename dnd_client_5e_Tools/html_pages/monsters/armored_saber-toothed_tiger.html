Keen Smell.

The tiger has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell.

Pounce.

If the tiger moves at least 20 feet straight toward a creature and then hits it with a claw attack on the same turn, that target must succeed on a DC 14+1d4 + 12 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone. If the target is prone, the tiger can make one bite attack against it as a bonus action.
