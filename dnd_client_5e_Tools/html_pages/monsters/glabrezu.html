Innate Spellcasting.

The glabrezu's spellcasting ability is Intelligence (spell save DC 16+1d8 + 12). The glabrezu can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components:

  • At will: darkness, detect magic, dispel magic

  • 1/day each: confusion, fly, power word stun

Magic Resistance.

The glabrezu has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.

Actions
Multiattack.

The glabrezu makes four attacks: two with its pincers and two with its fists. Alternatively, it makes two attacks with its pincers and casts one spell.

Pincer.

Melee Weapon Attack: +9+1d8 + 5 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 16 (2d10 + 5) bludgeoning damage. If the target is a Medium or smaller creature, it is grappled (escape DC 15+1d8 + 11). The glabrezu has two pincers, each of which can grapple only one target.

Fist.

Melee Weapon Attack: +9+1d8 + 5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (2d4 + 2) bludgeoning damage.

Variant: Summon Demon (1/Day)

The demon chooses what to summon and attempts a magical summoning.

A glabrezu has a 30 percent chance of summoning 1d3 vrocks, 1d2 hezrous, or one glabrezu.

A summoned demon appears in an unoccupied space within 60 feet of its summoner, acts as an ally of its summoner, and can't summon other demons. It remains for 1 minute, until it or its summoner dies, or until its summoner dismisses it as an action.
