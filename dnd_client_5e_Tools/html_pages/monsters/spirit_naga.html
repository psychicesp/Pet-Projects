Rejuvenation.

If it dies, the naga returns to life in 1d6 days and regains all its hit points. Only a wish spell can prevent this trait from functioning.

Spellcasting.

The naga is a 10th-level spellcaster. Its spellcasting ability is Intelligence (spell save DC 14+1d6 + 11, +6+1d6 + 3 to hit with spell attacks), and it needs only verbal components to cast its spells. It has the following wizard spells prepared:

  • Cantrips (at will): mage hand, minor illusion, ray of frost

  • 1st level (4 slots): charm person, detect magic, sleep

  • 2nd level (3 slots): detect thoughts, hold person

  • 3rd level (3 slots): lightning bolt, water breathing

  • 4th level (3 slots): blight, dimension door

  • 5th level (2 slots): dominate person

Actions
Bite.

Melee Weapon Attack: +7+1d6 + 4 to hit, reach 10 ft., one creature. Hit: 7 (1d6 + 4) piercing damage, and the target must make a DC 13+1d6 + 10 Constitution saving throw, taking 31 (7d8) poison damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
