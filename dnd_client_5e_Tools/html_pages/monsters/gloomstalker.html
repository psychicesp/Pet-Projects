As a bonus action, the gloomstalker can teleport up to 40 feet to an unoccupied space it can see.

Shriek (Recharge 6).

The gloomstalker emits a terrible shriek. Each enemy within 60 feet of the gloomstalker that can hear it must succeed on a DC 13+1d6 + 10 Constitution saving throw or be paralyzed until the end of the enemy\'s next turn.
