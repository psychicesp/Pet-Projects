Keoghtom\'s Ointment

\n
\n
\nDMG\np179\n
\n
\n
\n
\n
Wondrous item, uncommon
\n
Minor tier
\n
\n

This glass jar, 3 inches in diameter, contains 1d4 + 1 doses of a thick mixture that smells faintly of aloe. The jar and its contents weigh \xc2\xbd pound.

As an action, one dose of the ointment can be swallowed or applied to the skin. The creature that receives it regains 2d8 + 2 hit points, ceases to be poisoned, and is cured of any disease.

Found On: Magic Item Table B
Source: DMG, page 179. Available in the SRD (as "Restorative Ointment").
