A cleansing stone is a sphere 1 foot in diamete' b'r, engraved with mystic sigils. When touching the stone, you can use an acti' b'on to activate it and remove dirt and grime from your garments and your pers' b'on.

Such stones are often embedded in pedestals in public squares in A' b'undair or in high-end Ghallanda inns.
