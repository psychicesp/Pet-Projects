An arcane focus is a special item\xe2\x80\x94an orb, a crystal, a rod, a specially constructed staff, a wand-like length of wood, or some similar item\xe2\x80\x94designed to channel the power of arcane spells. A sorcerer, warlock, or wizard can use such an item as a spellcasting focus.

Multiple variations of this item exist, as listed below:

  • Crystal
  • Orb
  • Rod
  • Staff
  • Wand
