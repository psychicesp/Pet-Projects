Food and other perishable items do not age or decay while inside a Chest of Preserving. The chest is 2\xc2\xbd feet long, 1\xc2\xbd feet wide, and 1 foot tall with a half-barrel lid. The chest has a lock, which can be picked with thieves' tools and a successful DC 15 Dexterity check. Smashing the lock or any other part of the chest renders it nonmagical.
