Vicious Weapon

\n
\n
\nDMG\np209\n
\n
\n
\n
\n
Generic variant, rare
\n
Major tier
\n
\n

When you roll a 20 with this magic weapon, the target takes an extra 7 damage of the weapon's type.

Note: According to the SRD, it is an extra 2d6 damage, although this is incorrect.
