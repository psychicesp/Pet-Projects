Armor of Agathys

\n
\n
\nPHB\np215\n
\n
\n
1st-level abjuration
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: Self
Components: V, S, M (a cup of water)
Duration: 1 hour

A protective magical force surrounds you, manifesting as a spectral frost that covers you and your gear. You gain 5 temporary hit points for the duration. If a creature hits you with a melee attack while you have these hit points, the creature takes 5 cold damage.

At Higher Levels.

When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, both the temporary hit points and the cold damage increase by 5 for each slot level above 1st.

Classes: Warlock
Subclasses: Clockwork Soul Sorcerer, Conquest Paladin, Giant Soul (UA) (Frost) Sorcerer
Subclasses (legacy): Conquest (UA) Paladin, Conquest v2 (UA) Paladin
Races: Dwarf (Mark of Warding), Tiefling (Levistus), Tiefling (Levistus)
Source: PHB, page 215
