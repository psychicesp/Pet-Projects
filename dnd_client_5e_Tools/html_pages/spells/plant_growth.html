Plant Growth

\n
\n
\nPHB\np266\n
\n
\n
3rd-level transmutation
Casting Time: 1 action or 8 hours
Range: 150 feet
Components: V, S
Duration: Instantaneous

This spell channels vitality into plants within a specific area. There are two possible uses for the spell, granting either immediate or long-term benefits.

If you cast this spell using 1 action, choose a point within range. All normal plants in a 100-foot radius centered on that point become thick and overgrown. A creature moving through the area must spend 4 feet of movement for every 1 foot it moves.

You can exclude one or more areas of any size within the spell's area from being affected.

If you cast this spell over 8 hours, you enrich the land. All plants in a half-mile radius centered on a point within range become enriched for 1 year. The plants yield twice the normal amount of food when harvested.

Classes: Bard, Druid, Ranger
Subclasses: Ancients Paladin, Archfey Warlock, Land (Forest) Druid, Land Druid, Nature Cleric, Wildfire Druid
Subclasses (legacy): Wildfire (UA) Druid
Backgrounds: Golgari Agent, Selesnya Initiate
Source: PHB, page 266. Available in the SRD.
