Water Breathing

\n
\n
\nPHB\np287\n
\n
\n
3rd-level transmutation (ritual)
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 30 feet
Components: V, S, M (a short reed or piece of straw)
Duration: 24 hours

This spell grants up to ten willing creatures you can see within range the ability to breathe underwater until the spell ends. Affected creatures also retain their normal mode of respiration.

Classes: Artificer, Druid, Ranger, Sorcerer, Wizard
Classes (legacy): Artificer, Artificer (Revisited)
Subclasses: Arcane Trickster Rogue, Clockwork Soul Sorcerer, Eldritch Knight Fighter, Land (Coast) Druid, Land Druid
Backgrounds: Simic Scientist
Eldritch Invocations: Gift of the Depths, Sea Twins\' Gift
Source: PHB, page 287. Available in the SRD.
