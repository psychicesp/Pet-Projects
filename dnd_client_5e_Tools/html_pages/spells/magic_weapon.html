Magic Weapon

\n
\n
\nPHB\np257\n
\n
\n
2nd-level transmutation
Casting Time: 1 bonus action
Range: Touch
Components: V, S
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 hour

You touch a nonmagical weapon. Until the spell ends, that weapon becomes a magic weapon with a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls.

At Higher Levels.

When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the bonus increases to +2. When you use a spell slot of 6th level or higher, the bonus increases to +3.

Classes: Artificer, Paladin, Wizard
Classes (legacy): Artificer, Artificer (Revisited)
Subclasses: Arcana Cleric, Arcane Trickster Rogue, Clockwork Soul Sorcerer, Eldritch Knight Fighter, Forge Cleric, Glory Paladin, War Cleric, Stone (UA) Sorcerer, Zeal (PSA) Cleric
Subclasses (legacy): Forge (UA) Cleric, Hexblade (UA) Warlock
Optional/Variant Classes: Ranger, Sorcerer
Optional/Variant Classes (legacy): Ranger
Races: Human (Mark of Making)
Source: PHB, page 257. Available in the SRD.
