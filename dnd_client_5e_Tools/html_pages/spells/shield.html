Shield

\n
\n
\nPHB\np275\n
\n
\n
1st-level abjuration
Casting Time: 1 reaction, which you take when you are hit by an attack or targeted by the magic missile spell
Range: Self
Components: V, S
Duration: 1 round

An invisible barrier of magical force appears and protects you. Until the start of your next turn, you have a +5 bonus to AC, including against the triggering attack, and you take no damage from magic missile.

Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard
Subclasses: Arcane Trickster Rogue, Artillerist Artificer, Battle Smith Artificer, Clockwork Soul Sorcerer, Eldritch Knight Fighter, Hexblade Warlock
Subclasses (legacy): Armorer (UA) Artificer, Forge (UA) Cleric, Hexblade (UA) Warlock, Redemption (UA) Paladin, Artillerist Artificer (Revisited)
Races: Gith (Githzerai), Gith (Githzerai), Human (Mark of Sentinel)
Source: PHB, page 275. Available in the SRD.
