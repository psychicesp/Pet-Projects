Spider Climb

\n
\n
\nPHB\np277\n
\n
\n
2nd-level transmutation
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: Touch
Components: V, S, M (a drop of bitumen and a spider)
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 hour

Until the spell ends, one willing creature you touch gains the ability to move up, down, and across vertical surfaces and upside down along ceilings, while leaving its hands free. The target also gains a climbing speed equal to its walking speed.

Classes: Artificer, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
Classes (legacy): Artificer, Artificer (Revisited)
Subclasses: Arcane Trickster Rogue, Clockwork Soul Sorcerer, Eldritch Knight Fighter, Land (Forest) Druid, Land (Mountain) Druid, Land (Underdark) Druid, Land Druid
Races: Tiefling (Abyssal)
Backgrounds: Golgari Agent
Source: PHB, page 277. Available in the SRD.
