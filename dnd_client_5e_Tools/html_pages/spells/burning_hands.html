Burning Hands

\n
\n
\nPHB\np220\n
\n
\n
1st-level evocation
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: Self (15-foot cone)
Components: V, S
Duration: Instantaneous

As you hold your hands with thumbs touching and fingers spread, a thin sheet of flames shoots forth from your outstretched fingertips. Each creature in a 15-foot cone must make a Dexterity saving throw. A creature takes 3d6 fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

The fire ignites any flammable objects in the area that aren\'t being worn or carried.

At Higher Levels.

When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 1st.

Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard
Subclasses: Arcane Trickster Rogue, Eldritch Knight Fighter, Fiend Warlock, Four Elements Monk, Genie (Marid) Warlock, Light Cleric, Sun Soul Monk, Wildfire Druid, Giant Soul (UA) (Fire) Sorcerer
Subclasses (legacy): Celestial (UA) Warlock, Genie (UA) (Marid) Warlock, Genie (UA) Warlock, Undying Light (UA) Warlock
Races: Genasi (Fire), Tiefling (Abyssal), Tiefling (Mephistopheles), Tiefling (Variant)
Backgrounds: Rakdos Cultist
Source: PHB, page 220. Available in the SRD.
