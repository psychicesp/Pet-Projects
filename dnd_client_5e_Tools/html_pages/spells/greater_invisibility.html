Greater Invisibility

\n
\n
\nPHB\np246\n
\n
\n
4th-level illusion
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: Touch
Components: V, S
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute

You or a creature you touch becomes invisible until the spell ends. Anything the target is wearing or carrying is invisible as long as it is on the target\'s person.

Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Wizard
Subclasses: Arcane Trickster Rogue, Archfey Warlock, Armorer Artificer, Eldritch Knight Fighter, Genie Warlock, Gloom Stalker Ranger, Gloom Stalker Ranger (Revised), Land (Underdark) Druid, Land Druid, Twilight Cleric, Undead Warlock, Deep Stalker Conclave Ranger (Revised), Treachery (UA) Paladin
Subclasses (legacy): Armorer (UA) Artificer, Deep Stalker (UA) Ranger, Genie (UA) (Djinni) Warlock, Genie (UA) Warlock, Twilight (UA) Cleric, Undead (UA) Warlock
