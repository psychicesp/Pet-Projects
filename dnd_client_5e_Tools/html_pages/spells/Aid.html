\nPHB\np211\n
\n

2nd-level abjuration
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 30 feet
Components: V, S, M (a tiny strip of white cloth)
Duration: 8 hours

Your spell bolsters your allies with toughness and resolve. Choose up to three creatures within range. Each target's hit point maximum and current hit points increase by 5 for the duration.

At Higher Levels.

When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, a target's hit points increase by an additional 5 for each slot level above 2nd.

Classes: Artificer, Cleric, Paladin
Classes (legacy): Artificer, Artificer (Revisited)
Subclasses: Clockwork Soul Sorcerer, Divine Soul Sorcerer, Peace Cleric, Protection (UA) Cleric, Solidarity (PSA) Cleric
Subclasses (legacy): Favored Soul v2 (UA) Sorcerer, Favored Soul v3 (UA) Sorcerer, Unity (UA) Cleric
Optional/Variant Classes: Bard, Ranger
Optional/Variant Classes (legacy): Bard, Ranger
Races: Halfling (Mark of Hospitality)
Backgrounds: Boros Legionnaire, Selesnya Initiate
Source: PHB, page 211. Available in the SRD.
