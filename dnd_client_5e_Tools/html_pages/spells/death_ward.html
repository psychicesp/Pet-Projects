Death Ward

\n
\n
\nPHB\np230\n
\n
\n
4th-level abjuration
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: Touch
Components: V, S
Duration: 8 hours

You touch a creature and grant it a measure of protection from death.

The first time the target would drop to 0 hit points as a result of taking damage, the target instead drops to 1 hit point, and the spell ends.

If the spell is still in effect when the target is subjected to an effect that would kill it instantaneously without dealing damage, that effect is instead negated against the target, and the spell ends.

Classes: Cleric, Paladin
Classes (legacy): Artificer
Subclasses: Alchemist Artificer, Death Cleric, Divine Soul Sorcerer, Grave Cleric, Life Cleric, Undead Warlock, Undying Warlock, Ambition (PSA) Cleric
Subclasses (legacy): Favored Soul v2 (UA) Sorcerer, Favored Soul v3 (UA) Sorcerer, Grave (UA) Cleric, Undead (UA) Warlock, Alchemist Artificer (Revisited)
Optional/Variant Classes (legacy): Ranger
Races: Human (Mark of Sentinel)
Backgrounds: Boros Legionnaire, Orzhov Representative
Source: PHB, page 230. Available in the SRD.
