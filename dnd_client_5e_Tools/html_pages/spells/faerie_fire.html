Faerie Fire

\n
\n
\nPHB\np239\n
\n
\n
1st-level evocation
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 60 feet
Components: V
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute

Each object in a 20-foot cube within range is outlined in blue, green, or violet light (your choice). Any creature in the area when the spell is cast is also outlined in light if it fails a Dexterity saving throw. For the duration, objects and affected creatures shed dim light in a 10-foot radius.

Any attack roll against an affected creature or object has advantage if the attacker can see it, and the affected creature or object can't benefit from being invisible.

Classes: Artificer, Bard, Druid
Classes (legacy): Artificer (Revisited)
Subclasses: Archfey Warlock, Light Cleric, Swarmkeeper Ranger, Swarmkeeper Ranger (Revised), Swarmkeeper Ranger (Spell-less), Twilight Cleric
Subclasses (legacy): Swarmkeeper (UA) Ranger, Swarmkeeper (UA) Ranger (Revised), Twilight (UA) Cleric
Races: Elf (Drow), Half-Elf (Drow Descent), Half-Orc (Mark of Finding), Human (Mark of Finding)
Source: PHB, page 239. Available in the SRD.
