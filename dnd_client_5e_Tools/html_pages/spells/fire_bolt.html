Fire Bolt

\n
\n
\nPHB\np242\n
\n
\n
Evocation cantrip
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 120 feet
Components: V, S
Duration: Instantaneous

You hurl a mote of fire at a creature or object within range. Make a ranged spell attack against the target. On a hit, the target takes 1d10 fire damage. A flammable object hit by this spell ignites if it isn't being worn or carried.

This spell\'s damage increases by 1d10 when you reach 5th level (2d10), 11th level (3d10), and 17th level (4d10).

Classes: Artificer, Sorcerer, Wizard
Classes (legacy): Artificer (Revisited)
Subclasses: Arcana Cleric, Arcane Trickster Rogue, Eldritch Knight Fighter, Giant Soul (UA) (Fire) Sorcerer
Races: Elf (Eladrin), Elf (High)
Backgrounds: Boros Legionnaire, Gruul Anarch, Rakdos Cultist
Source: PHB, page 242. Available in the SRD.
