1st-level enchantment
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 30 feet
Components: V, S, M (a drop of blood)
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute

Up to three creatures of your choice that you can see within range must make Charisma saving throws. Whenever a target that fails this saving throw makes an attack roll or a saving throw before the spell ends, the target must roll a d4 and subtract the number rolled from the attack roll or saving throw.

At Higher Levels.

When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 1st.

Classes: Bard, Cleric
Subclasses: Divine Soul Sorcerer, Grave Cleric, Undead Warlock, Vengeance Paladin, Ambition (PSA) Cleric, Onomancy (UA) Wizard
Subclasses (legacy): Favored Soul v2 (UA) Sorcerer, Favored Soul v3 (UA) Sorcerer, Grave (UA) Cleric, Undead (UA) Warlock
Eldritch Invocations: Thief of Five Fates
Source: PHB, page 216. Available in the SRD.
