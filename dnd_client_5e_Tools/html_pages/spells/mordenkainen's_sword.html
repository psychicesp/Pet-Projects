Mordenkainen\'s Sword

\n
\n
\nPHB\np262\n
\n
\n
7th-level evocation
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 60 feet
Components: V, S, M (a miniature platinum sword with a grip and pommel of copper and zinc, worth 250 gp)
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute

You create a sword-shaped plane of force that hovers within range. It lasts for the duration.

When the sword appears, you make a melee spell attack against a target of your choice within 5 feet of the sword. On a hit, the target takes 3d10 force damage. Until the spell ends, you can use a bonus action on each of your turns to move the sword up to 20 feet to a spot you can see and repeat this attack against the same target or a different one.

Classes: Bard, Wizard
Subclasses: Arcana Cleric, Arcane Trickster Rogue, Eldritch Knight Fighter
Note: Both the Fighter (Eldritch Knight) and the Rogue (Arcane Trickster) spell lists include all Wizard spells. Spells of 5th level or higher may be cast with the aid of a spell scroll or similar.
Source: PHB, page 262. Available in the SRD (as "Arcane Sword").
