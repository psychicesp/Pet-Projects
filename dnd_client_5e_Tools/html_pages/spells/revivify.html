Revivify

\n
\n
\nPHB\np272\n
\n
\n
3rd-level necromancy
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: Touch
Components: V, S, M (diamonds worth 300 gp, which the spell consumes)
Duration: Instantaneous

You touch a creature that has died within the last minute. That creature returns to life with 1 hit point. This spell can't return to life a creature that has died of old age, nor can it restore any missing body parts.

Classes: Artificer, Cleric, Paladin
Classes (legacy): Artificer, Artificer (Revisited)
Subclasses: Celestial Warlock, Divine Soul Sorcerer, Grave Cleric, Life Cleric, Wildfire Druid
Subclasses (legacy): Celestial (UA) Warlock, Favored Soul v2 (UA) Sorcerer, Favored Soul v3 (UA) Sorcerer, Grave (UA) Cleric
Optional/Variant Classes: Druid, Ranger
Optional/Variant Classes (legacy): Druid, Ranger
Source: PHB, page 272. Available in the SRD.
