Greater Restoration

\n
\n
\nPHB\np246\n
\n
\n
5th-level abjuration
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: Touch
Components: V, S, M (diamond dust worth at least 100 gp, which the spell consumes)
Duration: Instantaneous

You imbue a creature you touch with positive energy to undo a debilitating effect. You can reduce the target\'s exhaustion level by one, or end one of the following effects on the target:

  • One effect that charmed or petrified the target
  • One curse, including the target\'s attunement to a cursed magic item
  • Any reduction to one of the target\'s ability scores
  • One effect reducing the target\'s hit point maximum
Classes: Artificer, Bard, Cleric, Druid
Classes (legacy): Artificer (Revisited)
Subclasses: Celestial Warlock, Clockwork Soul Sorcerer, Divine Soul Sorcerer, Peace Cleric
Subclasses (legacy): Celestial (UA) Warlock, Favored Soul v2 (UA) Sorcerer, Favored Soul v3 (UA) Sorcerer, Love (UA) Cleric, Unity (UA) Cleric
Optional/Variant Classes: Ranger
Optional/Variant Classes (legacy): Ranger
Races: Halfling (Mark of Healing)
Source: PHB, page 246. Available in the SRD.
