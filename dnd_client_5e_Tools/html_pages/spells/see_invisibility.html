See Invisibility

\n
\n
\nPHB\np274\n
\n
\n
2nd-level divination
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: Self
Components: V, S, M (a pinch of talc and a small sprinkling of powdered silver)
Duration: 1 hour

For the duration, you see invisible creatures and objects as if they were visible, and you can see into the Ethereal Plane. Ethereal creatures and objects appear ghostly and translucent.

Classes: Artificer, Bard, Sorcerer, Wizard
Classes (legacy): Artificer (Revisited)
Subclasses: Aberrant Mind Sorcerer, Arcane Trickster Rogue, Eldritch Knight Fighter, Twilight Cleric, Watchers Paladin
Races: Half-Elf (Mark of Detection)
Source: PHB, page 274. Available in the SRD.
