Freedom of Movement

\n
\n
\nPHB\np244\n
\n
\n
4th-level abjuration
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: Touch
Components: V, S, M (a leather strap, bound around the arm or a similar appendage)
Duration: 1 hour

You touch a willing creature. For the duration, the target's movement is unaffected by difficult terrain, and spells and other magical effects can neither reduce the target's speed nor cause the target to be paralyzed or restrained.

The target can also spend 5 feet of movement to automatically escape from nonmagical restraints, such as manacles or a creature that has it grappled. Finally, being underwater imposes no penalties on the target\'s movement or attacks.

Classes: Artificer, Bard, Cleric, Druid, Ranger
Classes (legacy): Artificer, Artificer (Revisited)
Subclasses: Clockwork Soul Sorcerer, Devotion Paladin, Divine Soul Sorcerer, Glory Paladin, Land (Arctic) Druid, Land (Coast) Druid, Land (Forest) Druid, Land (Grassland) Druid, Land (Swamp) Druid, Land Druid, War Cleric, Zeal (PSA) Cleric
Subclasses (legacy): Favored Soul v2 (UA) Sorcerer, Favored Soul v3 (UA) Sorcerer, Heroism (UA) Paladin
Races: Human (Mark of Passage)
Backgrounds: Dimir Operative, Simic Scientist
Eldritch Invocations: Trickster\'s Escape
Source: PHB, page 244. Available in the SRD.
