Prayer of Healing

\n
\n
\nPHB\np267\n
\n
\n
2nd-level evocation
Casting Time: 10 minutes
Range: 30 feet
Components: V
Duration: Instantaneous

Up to six creatures of your choice that you can see within range each regain hit points equal to 2d8 + your spellcasting ability modifier. This spell has no effect on undead or constructs.

At Higher Levels.

When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the healing increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 2nd.

Classes: Cleric
Subclasses: Divine Soul Sorcerer
Subclasses (legacy): Favored Soul v2 (UA) Sorcerer, Favored Soul v3 (UA) Sorcerer
Optional/Variant Classes: Paladin
Optional/Variant Classes (legacy): Paladin
Races: Halfling (Mark of Healing)
Source: PHB, page 267. Available in the SRD.
