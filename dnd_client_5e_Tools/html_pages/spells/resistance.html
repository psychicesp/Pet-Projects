Resistance

\n
\n
\nPHB\np272\n
\n
\n
Abjuration cantrip
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: Touch
Components: V, S, M (a miniature cloak)
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute

You touch one willing creature. Once before the spell ends, the target can roll a d4 and add the number rolled to one saving throw of its choice. It can roll the die before or after making the saving throw. The spell then ends.

Classes: Artificer, Cleric, Druid
Classes (legacy): Artificer (Revisited)
Subclasses: Divine Soul Sorcerer, Nature Cleric, Giant Soul (UA) (Stone) Sorcerer
Subclasses (legacy): Favored Soul v2 (UA) Sorcerer, Favored Soul v3 (UA) Sorcerer
Source: PHB, page 272. Available in the SRD.
